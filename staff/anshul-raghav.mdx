Anshul Raghav is a sophomore at Redmond High School. As the President of Innovative Minds, he hopes to provide accessible computer science education to students of underprivileged backgrounds. Anshul believes App Dev League is one of the first steps toward a bright future for diversifying the computer science community and giving everyone a voice.
